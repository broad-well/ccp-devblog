The Second-Person Dating Study

Group 14 · Living ArtsEngine 2021-2022

Welcome. Our Collaborative Creative Project will address some of three themes: Community/Isolation, Grind, and Futurity. In this development blog, we will document our process of creating and demonstrating an idea that addresses these themes.

We are moving forward with the idea of a Second-Person Dating study, where we put pairs of participants on a mediated date where each participant wears a VR headset showing their partner's perspective. This idea underscores our common pandemic experience of seeing ourselves on camera in online video conferences and extrapolates it to the romantic realm. It also comments on the potential of connection and intimacy between partners without seeing each other, subverting normative expectations.
